## Meet Emily Selby, DPT

I'm a Doctor of Physical Therapy specializing in pelvic floor and orthopedic conditions. After years of working in traditional clinical settings, I founded WildHer PT to provide the personalized, unhurried care that every patient deserves.

## My Approach

I believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. Every treatment plan is tailored to your unique needs, goals, and lifestyle. Whether you're recovering from childbirth, managing chronic pain, or working to improve your athletic performance, I'm here to support you every step of the way.

## Education & Credentials

- Doctor of Physical Therapy, University of Maryland
- Board Certified in Women's Health Physical Therapy
- Certified in Pelvic Floor Physical Therapy
- Member, American Physical Therapy Association

## Why WildHer?

The name WildHer represents strength, resilience, and the wild spirit within every woman. I created this practice to empower women to take control of their health and embrace their bodies' incredible capabilities.
